Who Is This For

Every GST-Registered
Business in India

GST compliance is not industry-specific — it applies uniformly to every registered business across India. Whether you are a manufacturer, trader, service provider, or e-commerce seller, the obligations under GST law remain equally demanding. A single misclassification, a missed reversal, or a mismatch in returns can trigger scrutiny, interest, and penalties regardless of your industry or turnover.

The GST Health Checkup is designed for every business that wants to stay ahead of compliance risks rather than react to them after a show cause notice arrives. If you have a GST registration, this service is for you.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a GST Health Checkup?

+
It is a comprehensive diagnostic review of your entire GST compliance ecosystem — including your business processes, accounting entries, tax invoices, GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-2B, GSTR-9), ITC claims, and all other applicable provisions. We examine everything through the lens of GST law to identify errors, mismatches, and risk areas before the department initiates scrutiny.

How long does the entire process take?

+
The timeline depends on the size and complexity of your business — including the volume of transactions, number of GSTINs, and the period under review. We assess the scope during our initial consultation and provide you with a clear timeline. Our team works efficiently to deliver the report as quickly as possible without compromising on thoroughness.

Is my business data kept confidential?

+
Absolutely. Confidentiality is the cornerstone of our practice. All data, documents, and information shared with us are treated as strictly confidential and are used solely for the purpose of this engagement. We maintain the highest standards of professional ethics and data security. Your data is never shared with any third party under any circumstance.
🔒 100% Confidential — Strict Professional Ethics Compliant

What documents do I need to provide?

+
Typically, we require: GST returns data (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-2A/2B, GSTR-9), books of accounts (trial balance, sales & purchase registers), tax invoices, e-way bill records, and any other relevant documents. We provide a detailed checklist after the initial consultation tailored to your specific business.

What happens if violations are found?

+
Don't worry — that's exactly why this service exists. For every violation found, we provide a detailed Rectification Roadmap with step-by-step corrective actions, applicable legal provisions, deadlines, and procedures. We also guide you through the entire rectification process to ensure every error is cured properly before the department can raise any demand.

Can businesses from any state in India avail this service?

+
Yes — this is a PAN India service. We serve businesses across all states and union territories. Since GST is a central legislation with uniform applicability, our review framework applies to every registered business in India regardless of location. Data can be shared digitally, making the process seamless for clients anywhere in the country.

How much does the GST Health Checkup cost?

+
The fee depends on the size of your business, volume of transactions, number of GSTINs, and the period under review. We provide a customised quote after understanding your specific requirements during the initial consultation. Reach out to us and we'll be happy to discuss.

How is this different from a regular GST audit?

+
A GST audit (GSTR-9C) is a statutory compliance requirement that reconciles financial statements with GST returns. The GST Health Checkup goes far beyond — it is a proactive, preventive diagnostic that examines your business processes, documents, accounting entries, and compliance at a granular level across 12 specific areas. Think of it as a full-body health check versus a routine blood test.
